About

The Villa Mentone Hotel was built in 1866 during the Victorian era which is evident through the features within the Hotel such as the high ceilings and original covings.

Last time it was refurbished in 2017 and has been modernised throughout the 30 rooms Hotel with the guests' comfort in mind.​

This year - 2019 we started to up grade more rooms by taking guest feedback and our own inspirations to take it to the next level where guest will truly experience the comfort,uniqueness and leave by creating an amazing memory.

Within the hotel, there's Bar/Restaurant and number of lounges to relax in, you will be spoilt for choices.

It also includes free Parking facilities within the Hotel (subject to availability) and plenty of free parking off site.

The Hotel is positioned on the centre of the cliff, overlooking the Shanklin Beach providing a panoramic view of the Sea.

The views from the hotel is truly breathtaking, you just have to see for yourself to appreciate.

The Hotel has disable access and 60% of the rooms have sea view as well as the Dining/Bar&Grill area.

Outside we have built a Garden Summer House with decking facing straight onto the Sea.

We have room specifically designed for disable access with wet room and new facilities installed.

The location of the hotel is an ideal venue for wedding events, parties as it's so close and surrounded by the blue Sea. Please contact us if you have something in mind and we will work with you to tailor to your need.

All our bookings includes complimentary Full English Breakfast and continental and don't forget to take advantage of BIG DISCOUNT up to £50% off on ferry crossing if you book on our web-site or directly with us.

Our guests are the heart of our business and with this in mind, we would like to welcome you to The Villa Mentone Hotel to experience a personal and friendly service by our dedicated team. We aim to exceed your expectations and that's a pledge from all the team members at The Villa Mentone Hotel.

We look forward to seeing you soon.
